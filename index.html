About The Government

The Federal Republic of Somalia, is one of the most homogeneous countries, with an estimated population of more than 14 million people. Located in the Horn of Africa, Somalia is bordered by Ethiopia to the West, Djibouti to the Northwest, the Gulf of Aden to the North, the Indian Ocean to the East, and Kenya to the Southwest. The official languages of Somalia are Somali and Arabic.

Our Country’s core economic sectors are; agriculture with key exports such as dry lemon, sesame, and banana; livestock and fisheries with an additional number of global attraction sites, historical sites, pristine beaches, magnificent waterfalls, mountain ranges, ridges, and national parks.

Goverment Services

Somali Federal Government provides services to Somalia people live in the country
or abroad, here is among number of service every citizen has to right to get.

Education

Ministry of Education provide quality education service for development by enabling our citizens to acquire knowledge and skills to participate in the global economy.

Health

Ministry of Health, Federal Government of Somalia, provides health programs to Somali Citizens , among the service include; Malaria Program, TB Program, WASH Program, HIV Program

SO Registry.

SONIC provides admin of the domain name space for the country code of Somalia (.SO), the country code Domain name space in the public interest of Somalia & global internet communities.

Passport / Visa Services

Somali Immigration issues passport to citizens in a simplified procedure. Somali passport can be held every Somali citizen, Foreigners get visa from this agency

Judiciary Service

Providers registration , record, legal aid and legal awareness, public complaint and advisory, every Somali citizen has the right to get justice as Somali Federal Constitution orders.

Central Bank

Central Bank providing payments and banking services to the government, stakeholders, financial institutions, and other approved account holders
